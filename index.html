Folding Bike

Folding Bicycle, a folding bicycle is designed to fold into a compact form, facilitating transport and storage. So bike can be easily carried into buildings, on public transportation, and more easily stored in compact living quarters or aboard a car, boat or plane.

Road Bike

Road Bicycle, Road bikes often designed to take you as far and as fast as your legs can manage. The road bike gets its name from the terrain / tarmac it is designed to be used on.

Best Roadies







Touring Bike

Touring Bicycle, as its name suggested, touring means self-contained cycling trips for pleasure, adventure, and autonomy rather than sport, commuting, or exercise. Touring can range from single-to multi-day trips, even years.

Best Tourers





Mountain Bike

Mountain Bicycle, is often built for sport of riding bicycles off-road, rough terrain, using specially designed frames. Mountain bikes incorporate features designed to enhance durability and performance in rough terrain. Mountain biking can generally be broken down into multiple categories: cross country, trail riding, mountain, downhill, freeride and dirt jumping.

Depends on cycling events, one can choose:

  • road-bike - for faster and longer distance ride. It can be used as touring bike as well.
  • folding bike - easy to carry and pack type of travel. many users use it as community transport. easy to store.
  • touring bike - due to its frame built to carry more racks which allow to put panniers. enable more comfortable seat posture that allow longer travel hours and distance.
  • mountain bike - built to withstand tough terrains, rocky road, mountain & hill climbing.
  • hybrid bike - kind of fall between road and touring bike.
